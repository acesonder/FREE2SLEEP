# Welcome to FREE2SLEEP - Getting Started Guide

## Welcome! 🏠

Thank you for accessing the FREE2SLEEP Crisis Centre website. This guide will help you navigate our services and get the support you need.

## What is FREE2SLEEP?

FREE2SLEEP is Cobourg's first dedicated crisis centre, providing:
- **Emergency Shelter** - Safe beds available 24/7
- **Support Services** - Case management, counseling, and advocacy
- **Hygiene Programs** - Shower and laundry facilities
- **Community Connections** - Referrals to local service providers
- **Crisis Support** - Immediate assistance when you need it most

---

## Quick Start Guide

### 🚨 In an Emergency
**Call our 24/7 Crisis Line: 1-866-995-9933**

If you're experiencing a life-threatening emergency, call 911 immediately.

### 🏠 Need Shelter Tonight?
1. Go to the [Bed Intake Form](../bed-intake.html)
2. Fill out the required information
3. We'll respond as quickly as possible
4. Or call: 1-866-995-9933 for immediate assistance

### 🚿 Accessing Hygiene Programs
#### Shower Program
- Visit [Shower Program Sign Up](../shower-program.html)
- Choose your preferred date and time
- Free towels and hygiene products provided
- Available daily 7 AM - 9 PM

#### Laundry Services
- Visit [Laundry Program Registration](../laundry-program.html)
- Select your preferred day and time slot
- Free detergent provided
- Open Monday-Saturday, 8 AM - 6 PM

### 💼 Case Management Support
If you need ongoing support:
1. Complete the [Client Intake Assessment](../client-intake.html)
2. A case manager will be assigned to you within 48 hours
3. They will help you create a personalized plan
4. Regular check-ins to support your goals

## Available Services & How to Use Them

### 1. Emergency Bed Intake
**Purpose**: Request emergency shelter accommodation

**How to Use**:
1. Navigate to [Bed Intake](../bed-intake.html)
2. Fill out personal information
3. Provide arrival date and estimated stay
4. Note any medical conditions or special needs
5. Submit the form

**Response Time**: Immediate for emergencies, within 24 hours for planned stays

---

### 2. Client Intake Assessment
**Purpose**: Comprehensive assessment for personalized support

**How to Use**:
1. Visit [Client Intake Assessment](../client-intake.html)
2. Complete all sections:
   - Personal Information
   - Current Situation
   - Services Needed
3. Be as detailed as possible - this helps us serve you better
4. Submit the form

**What Happens Next**: 
- A case manager reviews your assessment within 48 hours
- You'll receive a call or email to schedule an initial meeting
- Together, you'll create a personalized support plan

---

### 3. Service Referrals
**Purpose**: Get connected to community services

**How to Use**:
1. Go to [Service Referral Request](../referral.html)
2. Select the type of service you need:
   - Mental Health
   - Addiction Treatment
   - Medical/Healthcare
   - Employment
   - Housing
   - Legal Aid
   - And more...
3. Describe your needs and urgency
4. Submit the form

**Response Time**: 
- Emergency referrals: Same day
- Urgent referrals: Within 1 week
- General referrals: Within 2 weeks

---

### 4. Local Service Providers Directory
**Purpose**: Find community resources on your own

**How to Use**:
1. Visit [Service Providers Directory](../service-providers.html)
2. Browse by category:
   - Emergency Services
   - Mental Health
   - Housing
   - Food & Basic Needs
   - Healthcare
   - Employment
   - Addiction Services
3. Contact services directly using provided information

**Tip**: If you're not sure which service to contact, request a referral instead!

---

### 5. Case Management
**Purpose**: Ongoing support and coordination

**Services Include**:
- Individual assessment
- Goal planning
- Service coordination
- Documentation support
- Advocacy
- Regular follow-ups

**How to Access**:
1. Complete the Client Intake Assessment first
2. Visit [Case Management](../case-management.html) to learn more
3. Your assigned case manager will contact you

---

### 6. Instant Messaging
**Purpose**: Communicate with staff and case managers

**How to Use**:
1. Visit [Messaging](../messaging.html)
2. Select who you want to message:
   - Staff Support
   - Case Manager
   - Administration
3. Type your message and click Send
4. Wait for a response (monitored during office hours)

**Office Hours**: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM
**For Emergencies**: Call 1-866-995-9933

---

### 7. Document Sharing
**Purpose**: Securely share important documents with your case manager

**How to Use**:
1. Go to [Documents](../documents.html)
2. Click "Upload Document"
3. Enter document title and type
4. Select the file from your device
5. Add optional notes
6. Click Upload

**Accepted Formats**: PDF, Word (.doc, .docx), JPEG, PNG
**Maximum Size**: 10MB per file

**Privacy**: All documents are encrypted and only accessible by authorized staff and your case manager.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need an account to use the services?
**A**: No account is needed for most services. Simply fill out the relevant forms. For ongoing case management, you'll receive a Client ID.

### Q: Is there a cost for any services?
**A**: All FREE2SLEEP services are completely free.

### Q: How long does it take to get a response?
**A**: Emergency requests are handled immediately. Most other requests receive a response within 24-48 hours.

### Q: What if I don't have a phone or email?
**A**: You can visit us in person or have a friend/service provider contact us on your behalf.

### Q: Can I bring my pet to the shelter?
**A**: Please call 1-866-995-9933 to discuss pet accommodations. We work to find solutions.

### Q: Is the website secure?
**A**: Yes, all information is encrypted and transmitted securely. We take your privacy seriously.

### Q: What if I need help filling out forms?
**A**: Call 1-866-995-9933 and a staff member can help you over the phone, or visit us in person for assistance.

### Q: Can family members or friends access my information?
**A**: No, only you and authorized FREE2SLEEP staff can access your information, unless you provide written consent.
