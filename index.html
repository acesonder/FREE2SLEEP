About FREE2SLEEP

FREE2SLEEP is Cobourg's first dedicated crisis centre, built from the Durham policy framework to provide comprehensive support services for individuals experiencing homelessness or crisis situations.

We offer emergency shelter, case management, hygiene programs, and connections to vital community resources.
